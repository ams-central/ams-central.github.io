About the Project

AMS Central is a comprehensive knowledge hub developed as part of the ongoing research project titled Beyond Awareness: An Action Research to Engage the University of Makati in Embodying AMS Culture. This project aims to engage students, faculty, and staff of the University of Makati in actively participating in and promoting the principles of Antimicrobial Stewardship (AMS).

The focus of the study is to go beyond merely raising awareness about antimicrobial resistance (AMR) and instead, to implement actionable strategies that encourage sustainable practices and responsible use of antimicrobial agents.

Led by a dedicated team of researchers from the Institute of Pharmacy at the University of Makati, this study is designed to assess the current knowledge, attitudes, practices, and perceptions (KAPP) surrounding antimicrobial usage and resistance among the university community. Through educational interventions and surveys, the team aims to cultivate an environment of shared responsibility in the fight against AMR, ensuring that the university is not only informed but actively engaged in antimicrobial stewardship.
